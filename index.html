Pro Tips

Make It Stick

⚡
Speed Shortcuts
Buy garbanzos and lentejas pre-cooked in jars. Pre-washed rúcula/espinacas bags. Boil 6–8 eggs at the start of the week. Tomate frito Hacendado instead of making tomato sauce from scratch.
🧂
The One Spice
Pimentón de la Vera transforms every boring roasted protein. It's the difference between "food" and "Spanish food." Use it on everything.
📊
Track for 2 Weeks
Use MyFitnessPal — it has Mercadona/Hacendado products. Scan barcodes for 2 weeks to calibrate your eye. After that, trust your portions and ditch the app.
💧
Water Timing
A big glass at 12pm, 2pm, 5pm, and 8pm gets you to 2.5–3L easily without thinking about it. The window before the fast opens is perfect.
📉
If Fat Loss Stalls
After 3 weeks with no change: first cut snack carbs slightly. Second: reduce rice/pasta at dinner by 20–30%. Never drop protein. Never drop below 1,400 (her) or 2,100 (him).
🎯
The Target
Aim for 0.5–1kg/week loss. Faster than that and you're losing muscle. Slower than that after 3 weeks of consistency, tighten the snacks. Slow and sustainable beats aggressive and miserable.
